frequently asked questions (FAQ)

orders & shipping
Q: Where do you ship?
We currently ship nationwide across the Philippines. International shipping is not available at the moment.

Q: How much is the shipping fee?
Standard shipping is ₱99. We offer FREE shipping for orders over ₱1,500.

Q: How long will my order take to arrive?
Orders are processed within 2–3 business days.
Delivery takes:
• 2–5 days for Metro Manila
• 3–7 days for provincial areas

Q: Do you offer same-day delivery?
Yes! We offer same-day delivery for Metro Manila customers via Grab/Lalamove. Please message us before placing your order to confirm availability.
